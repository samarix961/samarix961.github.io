Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Samarix Core.

What exactly does Samarix Core do?
Samarix Core is an intelligent engine for automated user/device management and seamless updates. It orchestrates connectivity, optimizes performance in real-time, and handles updates silently via a manifest-driven system‚Äîall while prioritizing your privacy with zero PII collection by default.
Is it really free? What's the catch?
Version 3.0.0 Beta is completely free for early adopters. This is an opportunity to help shape the product. Future releases may transition to a paid or subscription model, but current beta users will receive advance notice and potential grandfathered benefits.
How does Samarix protect my privacy?
By default, Samarix collects zero personally identifiable information (PII). No names, emails, phone numbers, or precise geolocation. Optional telemetry (if you choose to enable it) is anonymous, aggregate-only, and uses minimal events. You're always in control of your data.
Which platforms are supported?
Samarix Core is under active development with planned support for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. Our cross-platform architecture is designed for maximum compatibility, with platform-specific optimizations delivered through regular updates. Join our Telegram channel for the latest platform availability announcements.
How do updates work?
Updates are delivered via a secure manifest system. The app checks for updates automatically, downloads them in the background, and applies them seamlessly without disrupting your workflow. Rollback safety and atomic deployments ensure you're always running stable versions.
